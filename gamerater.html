About GameRater

The main topic of the project website is to provide a platform for users to rate and vote on the game of the year.

Project Objectives/Goals

  • Allow users to submit their ratings for different games.
  • Aggregate the ratings to determine the game of the year.
  • Facilitate discussions and debates around the best games of the year.
  • Provide a platform for gamers to engage with each other and share their opinions.

Target Audience

The target audience for the website includes gamers of all ages and backgrounds who are interested in discussing and voting on the best games of the year. This determination was made based on market research, including surveys and analysis of online gaming communities.
